TSEC-CodeCell

TSEC CodeCell is a committee of Thadomal Shahani Engineering College which works towards nurturing interest and developing coding skills. They conduct various workshops and seminars for beginners, introducing them to various languages and algorithms, and for advanced coders, inculcating a regular coding habit to keep their programming skills sharp and helping students build their resume and expand their skill set.
